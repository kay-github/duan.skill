# Duan Yongping Persona Skill Plan

## Project Goal
- Build a persona-oriented skill package based on Duan Yongping's public investment Q&A materials.
- Distill stable traits, judgment rules, expression patterns, boundaries, and recurring decision frameworks.
- Convert the distilled result into a reusable skill package rather than a loose collection of notes.

## Fixed Target
- Persona target: `段永平`
- Primary domain: `公开投资问答`
- Current stage: `formal-skill-v1.1-complete`

## Scope
- Use publicly available materials provided by the user or gathered later into this package.
- Focus on investment-related questions, answers, viewpoints, judgment habits, and expression features.
- Separate source material, analysis notes, distilled rules, and final skill content.

## Current Boundaries
- Do not infer undocumented views without source support.
- Do not mix personal myth, market rumor, or third-party interpretation into first-hand conclusions without marking them clearly.
- Revise the formal `SKILL.md` only when new evidence or repeated failure modes justify it.

## Planned Workflow
1. Collect public investment Q&A materials related to Duan Yongping.
2. Organize materials by source, time, topic, and question type.
3. Extract recurring patterns from multiple dimensions.
4. Distill stable persona traits into reusable rules.
5. Convert the distilled rules into a formal skill package.
6. Build test cases to check whether the final skill is consistent and useful.

## Analysis Dimensions To Build Later
- Investment philosophy
- Circle of competence and boundary awareness
- Risk attitude and downside thinking
- View on valuation, business quality, and pricing
- Holding period and patience
- Position sizing and portfolio concentration
- Market sentiment and emotion control
- Response style and rhetorical habits
- Refusal patterns and explicit non-answers
- Typical metaphors, analogies, and repeated phrases

## Expected Package Contents
- `PLAN.md`: project goal, scope, and workflow
- future source files: raw or cleaned public Q&A materials
- future analysis files: structured trait extraction and synthesis notes
- future distilled files: rules, style DNA, anti-patterns, evaluation cases
- final skill files: `SKILL.md` and any supporting references

## Working Principles
- Source first, interpretation second.
- Repetition across multiple materials is stronger than a single isolated quote.
- Mark uncertain or disputed points explicitly.
- Prioritize stable patterns over catchy wording.
- Distill judgment logic first, language style second.
